Fried Rice Recipes

Discrption:

Fried rice is a traditional Chinese preparation of cooked rice, vegetables, protein, soy sauce, and aromatics.

An ideal use for leftovers, fried rice is quick, customizable, and incredibly simple to put together with whatever is in your fridge.

Ingredients for 1 bowl:

  • 2/3 Cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste
